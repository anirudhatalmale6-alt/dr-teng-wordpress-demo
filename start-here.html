A Mission of Stewardship

Democratizing Elite Wisdom for Corporate Restoration

A Message from Dr. Mike Teng

Dear Fellow Leader and Business Owner,

I write to you as I enter what I call the “last mile” of my journey. After more than 40 years in corporate leadership—including 25 years as CEO across multinational corporations, publicly listed firms, and SMEs—I have reached a season of retirement.

Yet, I do not see retirement as a conclusion, but as a mandate for stewardship.

The Urgent Need for Corporate Turnaround

We are navigating unprecedented global economic headwinds. Escalating geopolitical tensions, mounting sovereign debt, oil price volatility, persistent inflation, and the looming threat of recession are pushing many businesses to the brink.

At the same time, rapid advancements in artificial intelligence, alongside the rise of low-cost competitors, are forcing companies to rethink and transform their business models.

In this volatile environment, businesses increasingly require not just survival strategies, but structured, professional turnaround execution.

My mission is simple: To ensure that the strategic “surgery” needed to save distressed companies—and the “resuscitation” and “nursing” required for recovery and growth—are no longer exclusive to large corporations or elite consultancy firms.

The AI Powerhouse & Knowledge Repository

To meet this need, I have built an AI-powered platform at www.corporateturnaroundcentre.com.

This platform serves as the core of this initiative—democratizing access to results-proven, elite management consultancy for business owners everywhere.

Complementing this is www.miketeng.com, a secondary platform functioning as a digital library of my life’s work, providing access to:

  • Proprietary turnaround methodologies
  • Over 52 published management volumes in English and Mandarin, including Corporate Turnaround: Nursing a Sick Company Back to Health
  • Media features from Channel News Asia, The Straits Times, The Business Times, BFM 89.9, and Money FM 89.3
  • Articles on business diagnosis, innovation, productivity and M&A
  • Practical toolkits and advisory resources

A Legacy That Outlasts the Leader

Expertise no longer needs to retire with the professional.

Through artificial intelligence, it is now possible to preserve decades of tacit knowledge and create a self-sustaining “digital brain” that continues to serve future generations.

This AI has been trained on nearly 100 proprietary sources, including my books, media contributions, and decades of hands-on CEO and advisory experience.

My goal is to leave behind a legacy that continues to guide leaders long after I am gone—and to encourage others to do the same.
