A Mission of Stewardship

Democratizing Elite Wisdom for Corporate Restoration

A Message from Dr. Mike Teng

Dear Fellow Leader and Business Owner,

I am writing this to you as I enter what I call the “last mile” of my journey on earth.

After more than 40 years in corporate leadership across various industries—including 25 years serving as a CEO for multinational corporations, publicly listed firms, and small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs)—I have reached a season of retirement.

However, I believe that retirement is not a conclusion, but a mandate for stewardship.

The Urgent Need for Corporate Turnaround

Today, we are navigating unprecedented global economic headwinds. Between escalating geopolitical conflicts in the Middle East and several regions, mounting debts across major economies, oil price spikes, persistent inflation, and the looming threat of a global recession, countless businesses are being pushed to the brink. In this highly volatile climate, many companies will urgently require robust turnaround strategies simply to survive. Furthermore, the rapid integration of artificial intelligence and new technologies, alongside the emergence of low-cost competitors, mandates that companies proactively transform their business models to remain competitive.

My remaining mission is to ensure that the strategic “surgery” required to save a distressed company, and the “resuscitation” and “nursing” needed to transform and grow, is no longer the exclusive privilege of the global elite.

The AI Powerhouse & Knowledge Repository

To answer this urgent call, my primary focus has been building an AI Powerhouse at www.corporateturnaroundcentre.com, which stands as the main attraction and highlight of this initiative to democratize results-proven, elite management consultancy for every business owner.

To complement this powerful diagnostic tool, I have also established www.miketeng.com merely as a supplementary collection of resources. This platform serves as a comprehensive digital library of my life’s work, offering direct access to my proprietary turnaround methodologies. It houses an extensive archive of my national press and media interviews—including broadcasts on Channel News Asia, The Straits Times, The Business Times, BFM 89.9, and Money FM 89.3—where I break down complex business survival tactics into actionable advice. Additionally, you will find my published articles on critical topics like business diagnosis, business model innovation, and mergers and acquisitions (M&A), alongside practical toolkits and details on my 30+ management volumes, including Corporate Turnaround: Nursing a Sick Company Back to Health.

A Legacy That Outlasts the Leader

Expertise no longer needs to expire when a professional retires. Through Artificial Intelligence (AI), it is now possible for veterans to capture a lifetime of “tacit knowledge” and leave behind a digital brain that is self-sustaining and accessible to all.

I have spent my recent years training this AI on nearly 100 proprietary sources of my own know-how, including my best-selling books, decades of global media insights, and primary CEO and senior management consultancy and turnaround expertise. My goal is to leave a legacy that serves you long after I am gone, and I encourage other experts to do the same: do not let your hard-earned wisdom disappear; pour it into systems that empower the next generation.

Holistic Corporate Health: Secular Strategy & Spiritual Resilience

SMEs today face corporate problems that are more complex than ever, and many of these macro-economic factors are simply beyond human control. When facing the terrifying prospect of bankruptcy and watching a life’s work potentially go to waste, business owners experience immense, paralyzing stress. Solving these challenges requires a dual-track approach:

Secular Methodology: The AI utilizes my proven “Surgery, Resuscitation, and Nursing” roadmap to generate deep-dive strategic reports. These analyses are designed to help you navigate economic doldrums, identify root causes, and point you toward the right professional human expertise.

Ethical & Spiritual Foundation: We recognize that true leadership resilience requires spiritual strength to stay balanced amidst deep ambiguity and turbulent operations. For those seeking inner peace, solace, and Biblical clarity in times of overwhelming crisis, the system generates targeted prayers specific to your business challenges. Finding psychological comfort through faith is a highly tangible component of overcoming severe corporate hurdles. You can bring these insights to your pastor or spiritual leader; even if they lack corporate experience, these tools allow them to pray for your specific situation with precision and depth. This is an optional feature; users who seek this help will receive the comprehensive secular report alongside targeted prayers and citations of appropriate scriptural verses to support their journey.

A Professional Framework for Wisdom Sharing

To protect the integrity of this mission and maintain this as a sustainable resource, please note our guiding model:

Individual Responsibility: While I have trained the AI to follow my knowledge closely, all AI systems can occasionally experience “hallucinations.” As the leader of your organization, you remain the final decision-maker and it is recommended to seek other professional help.

My liability for any outcomes or financial losses resulting from the use of this information is strictly capped at $0 (or the exact amount of your sustainability contribution).

Thank you for allowing me to walk this “last mile” alongside you. Together, we can stabilize our businesses, preserve our communities, and restore hope through wisdom.
