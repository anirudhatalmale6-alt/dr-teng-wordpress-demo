Corporate Turnaround Playlist

40 curated videos on corporate turnaround and transformation

The Corporate Turnaround Playlist is 40 curated videos on corporate turnaround and transformation. It is a proprietary learning collection built from the distilled insights of Dr. Teng’s extensive body of work on leadership, transformation, and organisational renewal. Each video translates complex management principles into clear, practical lessons enriched with real-world relevance, supported by engaging music and meaningful lyrics to make learning both impactful and enjoyable. Together, these curated themes provide leaders, managers, and consultants with a comprehensive, experience-based guide to driving sustainable corporate recovery and high-performance transformation.

Category 1

Leadership, Mindset & CEO Stewardship

This category focuses on strengthening leadership clarity, courage, and conviction. The videos help CEOs and senior executives reset their mindset, embrace accountability, and lead transformation with purpose and discipline. They reinforce the belief that turnaround begins at the top.
