What Leaders Say About This Book

Professor Philip Kotler

“Dr. Teng offers a promising and insightful programme for restoring life to troubled companies. His book contains a very important message for the many ailing companies in Asia and elsewhere, namely that there are workable diagnostic and therapeutic steps to cure the patient and restore health.”

Professor Philip Kotler, S. C. Johnson Distinguished Professor of International Marketing, Northwestern University, US.

Professor Vijay Mahajan

“This ground-breaking book expounds turnaround concepts such as surgery, resuscitation and nursing which are at the leading edge of turnaround management. A recommended reading for all business schools and executives interested in building strong enterprises.”

Professor Vijay Mahajan, John P. Harbin Centennial Chair in Business, The University of Texas at Austin, US.

Mr. Oei Hong Leong

“I would highly recommend this book to CEOs, managers and top civil servants who are trying to steer their organisations out of the economic doldrums.”

Mr. Oei Hong Leong, Chairman, China Strategic Holdings Ltd, Hong Kong.

Dr. Y.Y. Wong

“Corporate Turnaround, is a rare book. Dr. Mike Teng … is able to offer revolutionary insights that promise universal applications with total clarity.”

Dr Y Y Wong, Chairman, The Wyre Group, Singapore. Chairman, Pacific Business Economic Council.

Dr. David Ahlstrom

“Corporate Turnaround provides clearly the steps needed to turnaround a firm's performance in the challenging Asian environment, where numerous pitfalls await the uninitiated. A must reading for those interested in Asian business.”

Dr. David Ahlstrom, Department of Management, Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong.

Mr. Ronald M Bradfield

“Corporate Turnaround: Nursing a Sick Company Back to Health is a must-read for all businessmen whether the businesses they run are sick or not … Here is a book full of practical advice on how to nurture and regenerate an ailing company by someone who has actually done it more than once.”

Mr. Ronald M Bradfield, Academic Director Asia, University of Strathclyde, UK.
